About Us

ShariaWiz.com provides an online portal to give visitors a general understanding of the law relating to Sharia-compliant Wills and Islamic law of inheritance in order to provide an automated software solution to individuals who choose to prepare their own Last Will and Testament that complies with Islamic law.

The Site includes general information about Wills and general information about Islamic Law of inheritance, including providing visitors free access to the ShariaWiz inheritance calculator.

OUR MISSION

Our mission at Shariawiz is simple: to provide the Muslim community with a user friendly, affordable and expeditious Sharia Compliant Will preparation software at the fraction of the cost of retaining an attorney. Even better, our sofware comes from a trusted and credible source, a verified expert in this field.
